6. Next level – vim bindings ⚓

  • mnemonics are OK:
  • c: change
  • y: yank
  • b: back
  • just a thought adjustment

7. Orthogonal nightmare 😦 ⚓

  • hjkl don’t map nicely
  • need something still easily hittable for up/down
  • HL still kinda close

8. KNJE ⚓

  • jekn? eknj? nekj? yeah ok
  imap tn <ESC>
  vmap tn <ESC>
  noremap e k " up
  noremap n j " down
  noremap k n " next/search
  noremap K N " back/search
  noremap N J " newline slurp motion

9. 1.5 years later… ⚓

  • eclipsed qwerty speed
  • typing in comf, prose and messages feel nice
  • happy bigram accidents/hand rolling
  • the vim rotation is working fine
