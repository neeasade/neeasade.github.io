Nicotine

those darn pouches

Published 2024-12-30, last edit 2025-06-20
#sleep

A few times this year, I’ve fallen prey to those little nicotine pouches (velo). Nicotine is awesome for about a week, and then the experience flips and you find yourself in a limbo where you must increase the dose to experience the same effects – subjectively it also feels like you swap from an “up” feeling to a “relaxed” feeling when taking it.

Here’s my personal reasons to not do it:

  • stuffy nose all the time
  • makes caffeine less effective
  • messes with sleep
  • irritates the bowels
  • damages the gums
  • it’s impossible (for me) to use in moderation
  • “nic sick” can make you feel heavy, lethargic
