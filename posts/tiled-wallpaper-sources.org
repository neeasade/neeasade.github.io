Tiled wallpapers are quirky, fun, and smol. That last property means you can do fun things to them fast, like programattically apply image filters or [[https://github.com/neeasade/dotfiles/blob/a6bbfdad1fc8982bcedbb6e9449823b935d4a166/bin/bin/bgtint][tint]] them so some current colorscheme.

The following is mostly a scraping of this [[https://nixers.net/Thread-Tiling-Wallpapers][nixer's thread]], and also the [[https://rizonrice.club/Rice:Resources#Wallpaper_sources][#rice wiki wallpapers]] (a tiled subset). The archives are compressed collections hosted on [[https:teknik.io][teknik.io]]. This collection focuses /only/ on tiled patterns.

- [[http://www.colourlovers.com/pattern][colour lovers patterns]]
- [[http://www.heropatterns.com/][hero patterns]]
- [[http://www.stripegenerator.com/][stripe generator]]
- [[https://github.com/BenjaminHCCarr/PropagandaTiles][propaganda tiles]]
- [[https://thepatternlibrary.com/][the pattern library]]
- [[https://github.com/dkeg/bitmap-walls/][dkeg's bitmaps]]
- [[https://github.com/wallace-aph/tiles-and-such][wallace's tiles and such]]
- [[https://www.toptal.com/designers/subtlepatterns/][subtle patterns]]
- http://cs.gettysburg.edu/~duncjo01/archive/patterns/
- [[https://1-background.com/][1-background]]
- [[http://www.tartanmaker.com/][tartan maker]]
- +http://bgrepeat.com+ [[https://u.teknik.io/FAaP8.7z][archive]]
- +http://squidfingers.com/patterns+  [[https://u.teknik.io/QkorA.7z][archive]]
- [[https://u.teknik.io/JPHul.zip][vain's archive]] (eclipses tiles-and-such(?) and squidfingers)

-----

For non-tiled sources, I usually go to [[https://unsplash.com][unsplash]] or [[https://wallhaven.cc][wallhaven]].
