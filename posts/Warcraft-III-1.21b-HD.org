Have an old warcraft III installer and want to play the game in 4K @ 120hz?

regedit path:

#+begin_src
HKEY_CURRENT_USER\\Software\\Blizzard Entertainment\\Warcraft III\\Video
#+end_src

Relevant Keys:

- ~resheight~
- ~reswidth~
- ~refreshrate~
